Welcome to Southwest Florida USA Dance!

The Southwest Florida Chapter of USA Dance is one of about 150 Chapters nationwide

We pride ourselves on offering affordable dance lessons taught by professional instructors. They are unmatched in dance education expertise and in national and international championship performance skills. With more than five affiliate instructors, we have a good match for you.

We promote dance opportunities for beginner and experienced dancers through:


  • Affordable Group Classes for Newcomers and Beginners (and dancers that want to refine their foundational technique).

  • Beyond the Basics: Classes for dancers ready to take the next step in their dance education.

  • Dance Parties: Monthly social events where everyone at any skill level can meet and enjoy dancing together. See our calendar section for more information.

  • Formal Dances: Elegant evenings of dinner, live music and dancing. See our calendar section for more information.

  • Workshops: Designed to enhance technique, add a few new steps and learn amalgamations (putting steps together).

  • Showcase: Annual event for dancers to perform and receive feedback from professional instructors and adjudicators.


Check out our upcoming events, plan your month of dance, and get connected to the most exciting dance events and professional instructors and lessons in Southwest Florida.
