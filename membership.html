Membership

Join Southwest Florida USA Dance #6049



You can choose from Three Easy Ways to JOIN or RENEW your Membership in USA Dance & Southwest Florida Chapter:


  • Print off the Membership form, fill it out and mail it in with your check.

  • JOIN on-line via Credit Card at the USA Dance National Membership Portal. When joining there, designate the Southwest Florida Chapter # 6049 as your Chapter!

  • RENEW on-line via Credit Card at the USA Dance National Membership Portal. The link goes to your USA Dance national Membership Portal, where you can update your contact information, renew your membership, or print your Membership Card.

  • JOIN OR RENEW at our next Chapter Dance Party or Dance Lesson completing the membership form and paying your dues by check or cash.

All are welcome (member or non-member) to our dance education and events! USA Dance membership offers additional benefits to members, including discount admissions to events and classes offered by more than 150 local chapters across the United States.



Membership benefits

  • Reduced rate at our dance education classes

  • Reduced rate at our monthly dance parties

  • Reduced rate at special events, such as Black-Tie Ball

  • Reduced rate for participants and attendees at annual showcase

  • Free e-subscription to the national bi-monthly dance magazine, the "American Dancer"

  • Reduced rates at select fitness centers

  • Discounts on goods and services pertaining to dance from vendors all over the country as listed in the "American Dancer" such as License to Dance, Dance Couture by Anton, Dancer Designs, Rhinestone Dancer, Satin Stiches, Ltd., TNT Dance Designs, ARTCO Dance Studio, Dancesoft/Dance Partner.com, The Dance Store, Dance Shoes of Atlanta, Dance Shoes of Tennessee, Emerald City Dance Botique, Showtime Dance Shoes, The Dance Dresser, Toe2Toe Dance Shoes and Worldtone Dance

  • Members have the opportunity to compete in USA DanceSport amateur dance competitions around the country

If you have questions or if you have difficulty renewing on-line, please feel free to e-mail us for assistance at southwestfloridausadance@gmail.com

We welcome you with open arms to membership in our Southwest Florida Dance Family!
