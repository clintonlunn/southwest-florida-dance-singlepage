Membership

Members of USA Dance and non-members are welcome to participate in our dance education and social programs. As dancers develop their skills and social or competitive interests, USA Dance membership offers benefits to members, including discount admissions to USA Dance events and classes offered by more than 150 local chapters across the US.

As a member of Southwest Florida Chapter of USA Dance, you will receive a free e-subscription to American Dancer, USA Dance's magazine. In addition, you will receive discounts to USA Dance classes and social parties. Whether you are a professional competitive dancer, dance instructor, amateur or recreational ballroom dancer, a novice dancer or would like to learn how to ballroom dance there is a membership level for you in Southwest Florida USA Dance.

To join print out and complete a USA Dance Membership Application, specifying Southwest Florida Chapter 6049 as your local chapter.



Membership benefits

  • Free entry into one Chapter dance with new and renewal membership when enrolling in USA Dance (value $12).

  • Discounted rate at all monthly chapter dances. Current members pay only $10, whereas non-members pay $15 (Members save over $50 yearly on chapter dances.)

  • Discounted rates for the Annual Membership Appreciation Dinner Dance. Members save $5.

  • Discounted rates at Chicago DanceSport Challenge championship competition. Members save $5 on spectator tickets.

  • Free e-subscription to the national bi-monthly dance magazine, the "American Dancer".

  • Members receive generous discounts on goods and services pertaining to dance from vendors all over the country as listed in the "American Dancer" such as License to Dance, Dance Couture by Anton, Dancer Designs, Rhinestone Dancer, Satin Stiches, Ltd., TNT Dance Designs, ARTCO Dance Studio, Dancesoft/Dance Partner.com, The Dance Store, Dance Shoes of Atlanta, Dance Shoes of Tennessee, Emerald City Dance Botique, Showtime Dance Shoes, The Dance Dresser, Toe2Toe Dance Shoes and Worldtone Dance.
