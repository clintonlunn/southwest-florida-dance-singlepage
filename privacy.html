Privacy Policy


Protecting your private information is our priority. This Statement of Privacy applies to SouthwestFloridaUSADance.org and Southwest Florida Chapter #6049 of USA Dance and governs data collection and usage. For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, unless otherwise noted, all references to Southwest Florida Chapter #6049 of USA Dance include SouthwestFloridaUSADance.org and Southwest Florida Chapter of USA Dance. By using the Southwest Florida Chapter of USA Dance website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Collection of your Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information about you unless you voluntarily provide it to us. However, you may be required to provide certain personal information to us when you elect to use certain products or services available on the Site. These may include: (a) registering for an account on our Site; (b) entering a sweepstakes or contest sponsored by us or one of our partners; (c) signing up for special offers from selected third parties; (d) sending us an email message; (e) submitting your credit card or other payment information when ordering and purchasing products and services on our Site. To wit, we will use your information for, but not limited to, communicating with you in relation to services and/or products you have requested from us. We also may gather additional personal or non-personal information in the future.


Sharing Information with Third Parties

Southwest Florida Chapter of USA Dance does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties.

Southwest Florida Chapter of USA Dance may share data with trusted partners to help perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to Southwest Florida Chapter of USA Dance, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Southwest Florida Chapter of USA Dance may disclose your personal information, without notice, if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Southwest Florida Chapter of USA Dance or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Southwest Florida Chapter of USA Dance; and/or (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Southwest Florida Chapter of USA Dance, or the public.


Automatically Collected Information

Information about your computer hardware and software may be automatically collected by Southwest Florida Chapter of USA Dance. Links

This website contains links to other sites. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such other sites.
